**Introducing the AFH Conversion Planner**

If you’re an AFH provider, investor, or real estate agent, you know the drill: a property catches your eye, but turning it into a licensed Adult Family Home means juggling zoning, city permits, county rules, WABO, fire marshal, and inspections. It’s easy to get lost in the details.

**What if you could go from “I have an MLS number” to “I have a clear plan” in four steps?**

That’s what the AFH Conversion Planner does. You enter:

1. **Property or MLS** — The address or listing number you’re evaluating.  
2. **City & county** — So the plan respects local permit and inspection reality.  
3. **Your goals** — What you want in the next 30 days and in 12 months (licensing, build-out, sale, refinance, etc.).  
4. **Your profile** — Whether you’re a provider, investor, or agent, and how deep your AFH/code knowledge is.

At the end you get a **plan snapshot**: one concise summary of everything you entered, ready to share with your team, permit expeditor, or consultant. The tool also shows how an AI “agent cluster” (strategy, UX, systems, compliance) would use that snapshot—so when you’re ready to automate, the structure is already there.

**Who it’s for**

- **AFH providers** — Current or aspiring operators who want a clear path from property to license.  
- **Investors** — Anyone underwriting or exiting AFH deals who needs a realistic timeline and checklist.  
- **Real estate agents** — Anyone listing or selling AFH properties and wants solid talking points and next steps.
